Elephant

The elephant is the largest land animal on Earth, known for its intelligence and strong family bonds.

Cheetah

The cheetah is the fastest land animal, capable of reaching speeds up to 120 km/h in short bursts.

Lion

Known as the king of the jungle, lions live in groups called prides and are powerful social hunters.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest land mammals, using their long necks to reach leaves high up in trees.
